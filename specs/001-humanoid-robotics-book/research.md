# Research Findings: Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Book

## Module 1: The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)

### Research Topics:
- Detailed ROS 2 architecture for humanoid control.
- Best practices for `rclpy` integration in complex robotic systems.
- Advanced URDF features for dynamic humanoid models.
- Interfacing various sensor types (e.g., IMUs, force sensors) with ROS 2.

## Module 2: Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity)

### Research Topics:
- Optimizing Gazebo for realistic humanoid physics simulation.
- Advanced sensor simulation techniques for LiDAR, Depth Cameras, IMUs.
- Integrating Unity for high-fidelity visualization with ROS 2.
- Sim-to-sim data transfer and synchronization between Gazebo and Unity.

## Module 3: The AI-Robot Brain (NVIDIA Isaac)

### Research Topics:
- Deep dive into NVIDIA Isaac SDK components for VSLAM and navigation.
- Techniques for synthetic data generation and augmentation for perception models.
- Reinforcement learning strategies for humanoid locomotion and manipulation in Isaac Sim.
- Best practices for sim-to-real transfer with Jetson platforms.

## Module 4: Vision-Language-Action (VLA)

### Research Topics:
- Integrating OpenAI Whisper for robust voice-to-text in noisy environments.
- Architecting LLM-based cognitive planning systems for complex robotic tasks.
- Translating high-level natural language commands to low-level ROS 2 actions.
- Multi-modal fusion techniques for speech, gesture, and vision.
